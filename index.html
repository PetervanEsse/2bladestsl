Welcome to the blog and hub page of the 2Blades group in Norwich. We are one of the active research sites of the 2Blades foundation and are based in the Sainsbury laboratory in Norwich. Here we work with our colleagues to develop durable disease resistance solutions that are required to assure food security. This page links to research programs of the 2Blades foundation (see below) as well as blogs and curated literature pages from group members.

If you would like to know what it would be like to work in the 2Blades group, please check out our blog.
